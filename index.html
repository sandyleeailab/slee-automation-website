FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

GEO is the practice of optimizing content to be cited by AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews. It includes featured snippet optimization, schema markup, entity building, and creating comprehensive content that AI assistants use as authoritative sources when answering questions.

SEO focuses on ranking web pages in Google search results to drive clicks. GEO focuses on being cited by AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity. SEO gets people to your website; GEO gets your brand mentioned when AI answers questions. In 2025, you need both for complete search visibility.

Over 30% of searches now involve AI tools. People ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI questions instead of clicking links. If you're not optimized for GEO, AI won't cite you and you'll lose visibility to competitors who are. GEO ensures your business is the source AI recommends.

To get cited by ChatGPT and AI assistants: build entity authority with consistent information across the web, create comprehensive and factual content, implement schema markup, win featured snippets, get mentioned on authoritative sources, and publish original research. Our SEO+GEO package automates all of this.

Our SEO+GEO package includes automated content creation optimized for both Google and AI, automated schema markup implementation, scheduled blog publishing, automated AI citation monitoring, and automated reporting. You set it up once, and the system continuously works to improve your search presence.

SEO typically takes 3-6 months to show significant ranking improvements. GEO results vary. Featured snippets can be won in 1-3 months, while AI citation improvements depend on how frequently AI systems update their knowledge bases. We provide monthly progress reports tracking both.
